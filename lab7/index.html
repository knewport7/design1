Welcome to My Nature Page

Nature is a big part of life and is around us everywhere. I love spending most of my time outside enjoying nature around me. I enjoy hiking, biking with friends, sitting in a hammock, and fishing. Throughout my entire life I have spent most of my days outside with friends and family. It makes each one of my days better with the fresh air and hearing the sounds of nature around me.

I have noticed more about how I see the world when I spend my time outdoors. I notice that my day is better because I adventure to new places while I spend this time with either friends or family. I have seen much more things that I would have never seen if I spent my time indoors. The sounds in nature are always different as that is the same as each day is a new day. I love to end my days sitting outside watching the sun go down and the birds humming along with the sound of the wind breeze because it makes the end of each day peaceful.
