Types of Jeep Wranglers

There are three different types of Jeep Wranglers that all incude different packages. The different types of Wranglers incude the Sport, Sahara, and Rubicon. In addition to this every year there are special editions added on to these types of Wranglers. For instance the Jeep Wrangler JK has a different interior that is newer and a more updated version than the TJ, which was the addition before the JK model. Along with that there are different features that are also upgraded such as the body style of Jeep Wrangler. The Jeep Wranger Sport edition is the Wrangler with the least amount of upgrades. The Rubicon is the most upgraded version you are able to purchase of the Jeep Wrangers. The Rubicon comes stock with a better suspension and a more stable ride with other built in upgrades. This model also is made more for a better offroad experience.
